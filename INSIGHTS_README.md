# Insights Page – FinTrack

The Insights page provides AI-powered analysis and actionable advice on your spending, savings, and budgeting. It helps you understand your financial habits, spot trends, and optimize your finances with clear visualizations and smart suggestions.

## Features & Functionalities

### 1. Overview Tab
- **Safe to Spend**: Shows your income for the current month minus the total monthly contributions for all your goals. This is the amount you can spend without affecting your savings goals.
- **Goal Contributions**: Displays the sum of all monthly contributions set for your savings goals, along with the number of active goals.
- **Expense Trend**: Indicates the percentage change in expenses compared to the previous month. Positive means you spent more; negative means you spent less.
- **Spending Insights**: Automatically generated tips and alerts based on your spending and goals. Examples:
  - Alerts if expenses are up significantly vs. last month
  - Highlights if a category's spending has spiked
  - Warns if your safe-to-spend is low
  - Encourages you when you’re close to reaching a goal
- **Recommended Budget (50/30/20 Rule)**: Suggests a budget split based on your income:
  - 50% for needs
  - 30% for wants
  - 20% for savings
- **Top Spending Categories**: Bar chart showing how much you spent in each category this month (e.g., Food, Rent, Travel).

### 2. Expense Autopsy Tab
- **Discretionary Analysis**: Breaks down your spending into essential (core) and optional (discretionary) categories, showing the percentage and amount spent on each.
- **Base Survival**: Highlights fixed essential expenses like rent, utilities, and healthcare.
- **Category Trends**: Identifies categories with high month-over-month growth and provides actionable insights.

### 3. Weekly Waste Tab
- **Weekly Leak Detection**: Analyzes your last week’s spending in each category and compares it to the previous three weeks’ average.
- **Potential Monthly Saving**: Estimates how much you could save per month by reducing wasteful spending.
- **Category Breakdown**: Lists categories where spending increased, showing the potential savings if you revert to your previous average.
- **Proactive Alerts**: Notifies you if a significant leak is detected (e.g., potential $50+/mo leak).

## Visualizations
- **Bar Charts**: Used for top spending categories and income vs. expenses.
- **Pie Charts**: For expense breakdown by category (on Dashboard).
- **Line Charts**: For savings trends (on Dashboard).

## How Data is Calculated
- **Current/Previous Month**: Transactions are filtered by date to determine current and previous month’s data.
- **Income/Expense**: Sums are calculated based on transaction type.
- **Category Spend**: Aggregates expenses by category for the current and previous months.
- **Budget Suggestions**: Calculated as percentages of current month’s income.
- **Insights**: Generated by comparing current and previous data, and by checking goal progress.

## Field Guide (Accessible via Info Button)
- **Safe to Spend**: Income minus goal contributions.
- **Monthly Goal Contributions**: Total planned savings for goals.
- **Expense Trend**: % change in expenses vs. last month.
- **Spending Insights**: AI-generated tips and alerts.
- **Recommended Budget**: 50/30/20 rule based on income.
- **Top Spending Categories**: Bar chart of category-wise spending.

## Navigation
- The Insights page is accessible from the sidebar navigation.
- Tabs allow switching between Overview, Expense Autopsy, and Weekly Waste.

## Technologies Used
- React, TypeScript, Zustand (state management), Recharts (charts), Framer Motion (animations), Lucide React (icons)
